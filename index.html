Something New I've Learned Recently

The Power of v0 for Rapid Prototyping

Over the past few weeks, I discovered v0 by Vercel and immediately saw its potential for bridging the gap between technical and non-technical stakeholders. What struck me most was how it democratizes the prototyping process, allowing someone with systems thinking but limited coding experience to quickly visualize and iterate on ideas.

The learning curve reminded me of my early days in AdTech automation: the tool is only as powerful as your ability to think systematically about the problem you're solving. v0 doesn't just generate code; it forces you to articulate your vision clearly, which often reveals gaps in your own thinking.

I built this entire page using v0, treating it as both a learning exercise and a practical demonstration. The process taught me about modern web development practices, CSS Grid and Flexbox, and how AI can accelerate the feedback loop between idea and implementation. Most importantly, it reinforced my belief that the best tools amplify human creativity rather than replace it.
